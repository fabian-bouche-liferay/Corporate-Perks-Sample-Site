# Wellbeing Support

The wellbeing program provides resources to support employees' physical, mental, and emotional health.

## Current resources

- Three confidential counseling sessions per employee per year.
- Monthly mental health webinars hosted by certified specialists.
- Access to meditation and sleep support apps.
- Burnout prevention toolkit for employees and managers.
- Nutrition coaching sessions available at discounted rates.

## Confidentiality

Use of wellbeing services is confidential. Individual usage data is not shared with managers.
