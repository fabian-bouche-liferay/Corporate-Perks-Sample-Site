# Family Support Benefits

Family support benefits are designed to help employees balance work and personal responsibilities.

## Current benefits

- Discounted childcare services with approved local partners.
- Back-to-school support webinars for parents.
- Family counseling resources through the wellbeing partner network.
- Parenting community sessions hosted quarterly.
- Emergency childcare guidance available through the partner helpline.

## Eligibility

Availability may vary by country and local provider coverage.
