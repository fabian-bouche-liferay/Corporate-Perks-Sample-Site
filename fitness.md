# Fitness & Sports Discounts

Employees have access to preferred rates with selected fitness and sports partners.

## Current offers

- 25% discount on annual gym memberships with FitClub Partner Network.
- Free first month for new employees joining selected partner gyms.
- Discounted virtual yoga and pilates classes.
- Reimbursement eligibility for one sports event registration per year, subject to local policy.

## How to access

Use your company email address when registering on the partner platform.

For gym memberships, select the "Corporate Employee Plan" during checkout.
